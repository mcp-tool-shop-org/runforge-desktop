# Privacy Policy

**RunForge Desktop** does not collect, transmit, or store any user data.

## Data Collection
- **None.** This application does not collect any personal information, usage data, or telemetry.

## Network Access
- **None.** RunForge Desktop operates entirely offline. No network requests are made during normal operation.

## Local Data
- All training runs, logs, metrics, and artifacts are stored locally on your machine in folders you select.
- No data is ever uploaded to external servers.

## Third-Party Services
- **None.** This application does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Account Requirements
- **None.** No account creation or sign-in is required.

## Contact
For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at:
https://github.com/mcp-tool-shop-org/runforge-desktop/issues
